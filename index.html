I'm Muhammad Ilyas Bacha, a BIM Engineer and Civil Engineer based in Peshawar, Pakistan. I specialize in building precision digital models that bridge design intent and construction reality.

With deep expertise in Revit, Navisworks, and AutoCAD Civil 3D, I coordinate multidisciplinary teams, detect clashes before they become costly on-site, and deliver ISO 19650-compliant BIM workflows for infrastructure and commercial projects.

My work spans structural modeling, highway alignment, and parametric bridge design — always with a focus on accuracy, coordination, and delivering on time.
